Nomilliano's Pizzeria

Welcome to Nomilliano's Pizzeria, located in the Heart of Texas.

About Nomilliano's:

In 2021, a Baylor student by the name of Noah Miller developed a vision to cultivate a third-wave pizza reform throughout the Heart of Texas. Drawing inspiration from his favorite pizza parlors in the Pacific Northwest, he knew that developing a similar culture in Waco would lead to great things. Purchasing upscale ingredients from local farmers, Noah has manifested his vision through Nomilliano's Pizzeria; an authentic nod to Italy's best.
